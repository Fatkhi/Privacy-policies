Last updated: November 14, 2025

We do not collect, store, process, or share any personal data or usage data through our app.
This includes (but is not limited to):

No location data

No usage analytics

No cookies or tracking technologies

No data transmitted to our servers or third-party servers

Since we do not collect any information, we do not store any information—either locally on your device or on any external servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because no data is collected in the first place.

4. Third-Party Services

Daily Drink Log does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks that collect user data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Daily Drink Log does not collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

6. Security

While no information is collected or stored, we remain committed to maintaining a secure app environment and adhering to best practices for software development.

7. Changes to This Policy

If our privacy practices change, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. You will be notified of any significant changes within the app or through an updated version release.
